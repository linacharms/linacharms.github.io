About Us

We are committed to fostering a society in Bulgaria where equality prevails, and every individual is treated with dignity and respect. Our mission is to eliminate discrimination, promote inclusivity, and advocate for the rights of all citizens, regardless of their background, gender, ethnicity, or any other defining factor.
